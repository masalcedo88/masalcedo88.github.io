About me

I'm a Full-stack Software Engineer with 10 years of experience in Sales Ops and Business Development @ IBM. I graduated my MBA with a concentration in Strategic Corporate Finance, but I can't ignore my passion for Javascript, Python and working with MERN-stack apps. I've always been a curious individual who knows how to communicate effectively, always work towards team goals and stay productive, whether it's a normal day or under heavy pressure.

Some interesting facts about myself include that I speak Spanish, French and English with native proficiency. I love the electric-guitar so much that I used to sing and play rythm/lead for my cover-band which I greatly miss. My wife and I love to explore new hiking trails, we specifically adore Yosemite Valley, but let me know if you have an interesting hike in mind. And lastly, I'm a versatile gamer but these days I'm mostly on Smash Bros Ultimate and Apex Legends.
